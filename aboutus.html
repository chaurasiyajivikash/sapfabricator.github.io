Sap Fabricator

Sap Fabricator engineers are having experience of designing and manufacturing quality bulk solid handling equipments for nearly 20 years. We have our factory located in Dombivli and our associate factory near kalyan on murbad road with 45,000 square feet area give us the flexibility to meet the demands of our customers in a broad range of markets.

This flexibility allows us to custom-design all types of dampers, gates, screw conveyors, screw feeders, flap valves and cone valve.

We design the equipments according to customer requirement i.e give the customer what he needs to operate his plant more efficiently and economically.

That includes providing our customers with sensible technical assistance before, during and after the selection of equipment.

With this site you can interact with us for our various products, and its applications and your application and your suggestion for the products and its features etc.

We can cast most valves in cast steel.cast iron, IS 4896 WITH 300-350 BHN HARDNESS, stainless steel.
